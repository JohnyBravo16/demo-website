Brief about the video game industry


From the last two decades, gaming industries have significantly grown up. Hardware manufacturers started making consoles and computers that could push games further than they had ever gone before.

The new technology led to an explosion of 3D games, with more advanced graphics. From simple machine games like Pong or Pac-man to modern ones including Virtual Reality.
Industries arent's running out of fresh ideas. Engineers are still working to upgrade said VR and what is more - to create holographic images.


Different game genres

There are multiple game type. From shooters set up during World War II, through sports games, up to economy games alike.



ESport has become an important element of modern culture. There are many organized competitions, where players all over the world can take part. It is increasingly recognized as sport.

Multiple gaming platforms

Players can (or sometimes have to) use different platforms to play games, such as Sony PlayStation or PC.



ESport

Players teams can compete with each other to win a world-wide championship. The League of Legends is a good example.
