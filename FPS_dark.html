First Person Shooter


Fist Person Shooter games, known as FPS are one of the most important and popular type of virtual fun. Action games are currently the most popular genre of games, and FPS games form the most popular sub-genre within action games. FPS games feature three dimensional environments and are centered around weapon-based combat in the first-person perspective (something like player would see in reality).

TPS or Third Person Shooters are similar to FPS games, except that the player views the character they are controlling from behind.


Call of Duty World War 2

First person shooter game, placed in World War 2 times.

Counter Strike Global Offensive

Multiplayer first person shooter game, where player takes one side of terrorist-counterterrorist conflict.

Battlefield 4

Multiplayer shooter game, about modern world conflicts.
