Battle Royale


A Battle Royale game is a video game genre that blends the survival, exploration and scavenging elements of a survival game with last man standing gameplay.

Battle royale games challenge a large number of players, starting with minimal equipment, to search for weapons and armor and eliminate other opponents, while trying to stay in safe playable area which shrinks as the time passes, with the winner being the last competitor in the game.
